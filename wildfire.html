Wildfire Preparedness

Before a Wildfire

  • Clear flammable vegetation around your home.
  • Prepare an emergency kit and evacuation plan.
  • Install fire-resistant materials in your home.
  • Keep important documents in a fireproof safe.

During a Wildfire

  • Evacuate immediately if advised to do so.
  • Wear protective clothing to protect from heat.
  • Stay informed through local news and alerts.

After a Wildfire

  • Return home only when authorities say it is safe.
  • Check your property for hot spots and embers.
  • Document damage for insurance purposes.
